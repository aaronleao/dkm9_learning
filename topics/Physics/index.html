📍 Recommended Learning Paths

For Complete Beginners

Path: Mechanics (Beginner) → Electromagnetism (Beginner)

Start with basic kinematics and Newton's laws before moving onto static charges and simple circuits.

For Engineering Students

Path: Mechanics → Electromagnetism → Optics

Focus on practical problem-solving, forces, energy, circuits, and wave behavior.

For Theoretical Physics

Path: Mechanics (Advanced) → Quantum Mechanics → Research Topics

Master Lagrangian/Hamiltonian formulation, then dive deep into quantum mechanics and field theory.
